THE FOUNDERS

The Croatian design duo, Tomislav Trčak & Anthea Bebek, cousins-turned-business-partners, were both born into smalltown working families in suburban Samobor. Growing up in rather traditional households, the pair’s childhood revolved heavily around sports, with Tomo becoming national tennis pro at one point. Still, early on, the two bonded over mutual love for US arthouse films, pop art, and alternative music of the 90s. Their friends would smuggle foreign records and movies, which they spent days watching and listening. That is also where their love of fashion started - in observing the details, their idols’ styles, and the photography. They would constantly find themselves unsatisfied with their own wardrobes. Everything was either too tight or too big, or too “this” and very “that”, but not quite “there”. Years later, the bold duo’s first venture was an entirely digital collection, which later transformed into the Milli Point Two of today. Now, with the help of Anthea’s years of creative and design experience and Tomislav’s business savviness, the brand seeks to satisfy the youth daydreams about making clothes that, in Tomislav’s own words “their younger selves would kill to be able to wear”.
